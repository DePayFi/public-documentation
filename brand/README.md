# Brand

> Is a way of identification. It is how your customers recognise and experience your business. A strong brand is more than just a logo — it's reflected in everything from your customer service style, staff uniforms, business cards and premises to your marketing materials and advertising.

## Brand Name

DePay

The brand name stands for decentralized payments,  
is case-sensitive,  
based on and related to [DeFi (decentralized finance)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decentralized_finance).

In case "DePay" should be taken as a name on certain platforms we register, we usually fallback to "DePayFi" (without any special characters).
